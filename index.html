About Us

Bengaluru Azure User Group

We are a user group with 1000+ members of Developers, IT Pros and Architects dedicated to sharing knowledge with and learning from each other. We have a strong online presence and hold regular face to face meetings in Bengaluru, India. We leverage as much social networking and collaboration technologies as we can extend our reach all those geographically challenging places. The Bengaluru Azure User Group (BAUG) organisers do not work for Microsoft. However, we do truly appreciate the donation of personal (and unpaid) time that all our speakers, Microsoft Staff and MVPs give to support us.

Check List

  • When do we meet? We normally meet on the 3rd Saturday morning of every month.
  • Where do we meet? We don’t have a permenant home, we depend on partners, vendors who generously offer their office space to host us. Drop an email if you are intrerested in hosting us at your office facility.
  • How much does it cost? All session from the Bengaluru Azure User Group are free unless otherwise stated.
  • Is there food / drink? Normally user group events are catered for free via the way of sponsorship. Typically the food is Pizza and Soft drink.
  • How do I join? Simply click on the “Join our mailing list” in the menu above and fill out your details and you will automatically joined to the mailing list.
  • Are we run by Microsoft? No. However, the session we run are Microsoft focused and we do receive sponsorship from Microsoft and other vendors to cover the cost of putting on these events.
